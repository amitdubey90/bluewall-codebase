About

If you dont do what is best for your body, you are the one who comes up on the short end.

EatRight is a device independent fitness tracking application that takes complete control of your daily diet and physical activities by allowing you to set target goals and providing you with a daily planned diet. The diet plan focuses on user's physical activity level, taste preferences for food, food likes and dislikes and daily calorie requirements suiting the fitness goal. EatRight makes it easier for the user to focus on his diet by providing food recommendations.
